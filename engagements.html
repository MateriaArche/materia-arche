Three ways to work with Materia Arche

We keep early engagements narrow and useful. The point is to generate decision-grade output, not to create theatre.

Benchmark Audit

For teams with an existing ranking, screening, or candidate-selection workflow that needs a technical stress test.

What you receive

A review of assumptions, baseline comparison logic, risk points, and a recommended benchmark frame.

Candidate Prioritisation Sprint

For teams with a defined candidate set and a near-term decision to make.

What you receive

A ranked shortlist, decision rationale, uncertainty notes, and a proposed validation matrix.

Managed Evidence Package

For teams preparing a focused external validation loop or partner-facing technical package.

What you receive

A structured evidence package that connects candidate ranking, technical rationale, and next-step validation planning.

To discuss fit, send a short project outline with the material system, candidate count, decision timeline, and available data sources.
